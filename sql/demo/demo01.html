连表查询

常用的：SELECT * from role_menu rm INNER JOIN menu m on rm.menuid=m.menu_id where rm.roleid=5
升级的：SELECT * from role_menu rm, menu m where rm.menuid=m.menu_id and rm.roleid=5
表结果作为变量参数：select * from student inner join employment on student.st_id =employment.student_id where (select count(*) from interview where interview.student_id = student.st_id and isdel=1) <= 3
表结果作为新表查询：select eg.num IFNULL(eg.num,0) from month_aux m LEFT JOIN (select count(*) num,e.* from enterprise_aud e where e.user_id=#{user_id} GROUP BY DATE_FORMAT(cooperativetime,'%Y%m')) eg on m.m_id = DATE_FORMAT(eg.cooperativetime,'%m')
